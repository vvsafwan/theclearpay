Services

Explore a range of reliable payment solutions designed to make your financial life easier. Whether you're an individual or a business, we have the tools to help you thrive.

Powering Smarter, Faster Business Payments

FinzoraPay offers a complete suite of modern payment solutions—from secure bank linking and one-time payments to mass payouts, QR transactions, and point-of-sale tools. Built for scale and simplicity, our platform helps you move money with ease, integrate effortlessly, and grow with confidence.

Banking

Bank Link

Securely link customer bank accounts with FinzoraPay’s Bank Link service. Enable seamless fund transfers, balance checks, and real-time account verification to enhance user experience and trust.

One-time Payment

Allow users to make quick, secure one-time payments with zero friction. Whether it’s a bill, service, or purchase, FinzoraPay ensures a smooth, fast checkout experience every time.

Mass Payment

FinzoraPay simplifies large-scale disbursements with its Mass Payment solution. Pay vendors, employees, or partners in bulk—instantly, accurately, and with full transaction traceability.

Payment Connections

Easily integrate with banks, wallets, and UPI systems using FinzoraPay's Payment Connections. Empower your platform with multiple payment rails for maximum flexibility and reach.

QR Payment

Accept payments anywhere using FinzoraPay’s QR Payment solution. Just generate and share your QR code—customers can scan and pay instantly through their preferred payment apps.

Fixed QR

Generate a single static QR code for repeated use with FinzoraPay’s Fixed QR. Ideal for stores and service providers—no need to change codes for each transaction.

Live QR

Create dynamic QR codes with unique payment amounts and metadata using FinzoraPay’s Live QR feature. Perfect for time-bound or personalized transactions with added tracking capabilities.

Gateway Service

Process online transactions securely through FinzoraPay’s powerful Gateway Service. Accept payments from cards, wallets, UPI, and more with high uptime and fraud protection.

Point of Sale

Transform your business operations with FinzoraPay’s modern Point of Sale solutions. Manage sales, accept multiple payment methods, and track transactions—all from one device.

Verification

Personal Verification

Ensure accurate identity validation with FinzoraPay’s Personal Verification. Instantly verify customer details like name, date of birth, and contact data to reduce fraud and speed up onboarding.

Corporate Check

Authenticate business entities with FinzoraPay’s Corporate Check. From GST verification to company registration and ownership details, get the data you need to assess business credibility.

Smart Analytics

Gain actionable insights with FinzoraPay’s Smart Analytics. Monitor transaction trends, user behavior, and financial performance in real time to drive smarter business decisions.

Check Bundles

Run multiple verifications in a single flow with FinzoraPay’s Check Bundles. Combine identity, financial, and compliance checks into one streamlined process for faster verifications.

Finance Check

Access reliable financial data with FinzoraPay’s Finance Check. Evaluate income, bank activity, and account status to support risk assessments, credit scoring, or KYC processes.

Identity Check

Verify government-issued IDs like Aadhaar, PAN, or Passport with FinzoraPay’s Identity Check. Ensure your users are who they claim to be—securely and instantly.

Location Insights

Leverage geolocation data through FinzoraPay’s Location Insights. Understand user movement, verify presence, or detect anomalies using precise, permission-based location tracking.

Travel

Lodging Reservation

Simplify hotel bookings with FinzoraPay’s Lodging Reservation service. Offer your users a seamless way to search, reserve, and pay for accommodations—whether for business or leisure.

Coach Reservation

Enable easy intercity and intracity bus bookings with FinzoraPay’s Coach Reservation solution. Users can reserve seats, make payments, and receive tickets instantly with real-time updates.

Airfare Reservation

Provide smooth flight booking experiences with FinzoraPay’s Airfare Reservation. Users can search flights, compare fares, and complete payments securely in just a few clicks.

Rail Reservation

Offer convenient train ticketing through FinzoraPay’s Rail Reservation system. Integrated with national rail networks, it supports real-time availability, seat selection, and instant payments.
